My name is Andy Johnson.

I was born and raised in Kansas City. I'm currently finishing up LC101, a coding bootcamp designed to get people with non-tech-traditional backgrounds into careers in the industry. I hope to use my skills to make more websites like this one for the Kansas City clubs and nonprofits I love.

In addition to learning front end development, I also:

  • Write reviews of romance novels for a site called Fresh Fiction. I get paid in free books, which makes my English major heart happy.
  • Gig around town as a violinist -- mostly classical, though I'd love to learn more bluegrass.
  • Mountain bike (badly) and sometimes write about my experience with Kansas City's awesome, home-grown, supportive dirt scene.

To read my reflections on learning everything from HTML to dressing like a mountain biker to teaching myself content marketing and SEO, check out my blog.
